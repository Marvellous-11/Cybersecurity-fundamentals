# 🛡️ Asset Security & Risk Management

## 🔹 Overview
This project documents my learning and understanding of asset security, risk management, and cybersecurity frameworks.

---

## 🔹 Key Concepts Learned

### Assets in Cybersecurity
- Digital assets: data, systems, networks  
- Physical assets: devices like laptops and routers  

---

### Asset Classification
- Public  
- Internal  
- Confidential  
- Restricted  

---

### States of Data
- Data at rest  
- Data in transit  
- Data in use  

---

### Risks, Threats, and Vulnerabilities
- Risk: potential loss or damage  
- Threat: possible danger to systems  
- Vulnerability: weakness that can be exploited  

---

### Security Planning
- Asset identification  
- Risk assessment  
- Security controls  
- Monitoring  

---

### NIST Framework
- Identify  
- Protect  
- Detect  
- Respond  
- Recover  

---

## 🔹 Key Learnings
- Understanding how to classify and protect assets  
- Awareness of risks, threats, and vulnerabilities  
- Importance of structured security frameworks
